Our Menu

Chicken

Welcome to our world-class chicken dishes. From golden rotisserie birds glazed in honey and herbs, to slow-braised thighs simmered in a rich tomato-olive broth, our chicken menu is a celebration of bold flavors and comforting tradition. Every dish is crafted with free-range, sustainably sourced poultry and paired with seasonal sides that change with the harvest.

Try our signature Nashville Hot Chicken — crispy double-fried, coated in our secret cayenne paste, and served on toasted brioche with house-pickled cucumbers and a drizzle of wildflower honey. Or explore the lighter side with our herb-roasted chicken breast over a bed of farro, roasted cherry tomatoes, and lemon-tahini dressing.
